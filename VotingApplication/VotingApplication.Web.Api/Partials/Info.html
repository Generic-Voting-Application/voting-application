Single Vote (First Past The Post)

  • You can vote for just one option.
  • You can change your vote however many times you want.
  • The option with the most votes is the winner.

Points Based Vote

  • You have X number of votes to distribute over the options.
  • Each option can be voted for Y number of times.
  • You can change the distribution of your votes however many times you want.
  • The option with the most votes is the winner.

Ranked Vote (Instant Runoff)

  • You may select any number of votes and arrange them by preference.
  • The top option is considered your primary preference.
  • You can change the your preferences however many times you want.
  • The result is decided by multiple rounds of elimination.
  • Each round, the option with the lowest number of votes is eliminated and it's votes redistributed based on preference.
  • The option with a majority will be declared the winner.
